## Think twice about your domain name

Is it future-proof? Are you really happy with it? I've changed it multiple times and JVR.Cloud is finally a domain name I'm really happy about.

## Use a WordPress.com Trial

I'm not getting payed for this, but WordPress.com is absolutely amazing. It's fast, it has good SEO (Search Engine Optimalization, Google that) and requires no maintenance. SEO is important if you want your site to end up high in the Google rankings
