**Last week I visited the Avanade !nnovate 2019 event in Milan. This event brings Avanade colleagues, clients & guests together to share new ideas. !nnovate is a 2.5 day event filled with idea-sharing, learning and collaborative discussions. Back in April I was selected as one of the !nnovate Ambassadors from Avanade Netherlands to attend the event. With a group of 6 Avanadi we went to the beautiful city of Milan. In this blog post, I will share some highlights of the event.**

![Milan Cathedral](/images/2019/06/image.jpg)

## Day 1

The first official day of the event was on Tuesday. The day started with keynotes from speakers such as our CEO Adam Warby and our CTIO Matt Joe.

Regional leads from Avanade Italy like Emiliano Rantucci (GM) and Roberto Chinnelli (Regional Innovation Lead) were very happy to have this event hosted in Milan. I couldn't agree more - the city is beautiful, the food is amazing and one of the Accenture Customer Innovation Networks is based in Milan.

More of that later in this post.
