How cool would that be. A certificate which you can use to sign documents, with your fingerprint as a private key?

Imagine that the government will use your fingerprint in a passport and use that same fingerprint in a certificate which you can use to sign documents or e-mails.

When I see someone signing a document, I imagine how easy it is to manipulate the signature.

Signatures are only validated if you sign it at a notary if you bought a house for example.

It's kind of scary that someone else can copy your signature and approve something under your name.
