**Three months ago, I wrote a blog post about my [My Office 365 based personal, secure & unique email system](http://DevSecNinja.com/2019/05/19/my-office-365-based-personal-secure-unique-email-system/). In this blog post, I will tell you my experience over the last three months.**

## The Stats

Running the _Get-UsedMailAlias_ cmdlet from the PowerShell module tells me that I'm currently using 61 aliases: ![msedge_2019-08-10_10-52-03](/images/2019/08/msedge_2019-08-10_10-52-03.png) I've been using Azure Cloud Shell extensively with this module and I'm happy to say that it works really well from Windows 10, my iPad in Safari and the Azure iOS app.

## Writing emails from the aliases

I'm mainly relying on the Outlook Web App on Office 365 for this, as most mobile apps like Outlook or the built-in iOS Mail app don't allow me to change the "From" address. I don't email the 61 companies that often, but when I have to it can be a bit of struggle as I need my laptop for Outlook Web Access.
