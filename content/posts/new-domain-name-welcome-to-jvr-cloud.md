Frequent visitors of my blog may have noticed that the domain name of the blog has changed from jvrtech.net to jvr.cloud. You can still reach my blog on jvrtech.net, but within a couple of years, that redirect may disappear.

## .Cloud TLD

Most of my short nicknames or my full name aren't available anymore on the TLDs like .com, .net or .org. When the .cloud TLD was introduced, I saw an opportunity to buy this very short domain name. Tech in a domain name tells people that it has something to do with technology which I like, but Cloud will hopefully do that too. Thank you for visiting my blog! Cheers, Jean-Paul
