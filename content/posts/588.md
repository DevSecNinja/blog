I was studying for my 70-534 Azure exam and I needed to spin up a virtual machine to train some of the exam objectives. I always try to use my lab time efficiently instead of just installing and removing WordPress for example to test some features. In this blog post, I'll try to explain the benefits of OneDrive and OwnCloud.

### OwnCloud

**OwnCloud doesn't work with Windows Phone / Windows Mobile** I use a Nokia Lumia 925 as my primary phone, and I can't use OwnCloud with it. **OwnCloud allows you to manage your data yourself**  

### OneDrive

**OneDrive is plug-and-play, no management needed** I maintain and build servers every day, so when I'm at home I don't want to manage my own servers. That's why I'm using WordPress.com and Office 365 as well, instead of my own web and email server. I don't want that hassle to bring my server back up during the day when something happened.

**OneDrive has a very tight integration with Office** OneDrive works very well with Office 2013 and Office 2016. You can easily save and open files from OneDrive. I use Office Lens on my phone a lot to make pictures of receipts. This will only work with OneDrive/OneNote.

**OneDrive works on every big platform: iOS, Android and Windows** Just download the app, login and you're good to go.
